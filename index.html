Usage Questions & Answers

How do I use a grounding sheet?

  1. Prepare Your Bed: Remove your current fitted sheet from your mattress.
  2. Place the Grounding Sheet: Put the ECOHEAL grounding fitted sheet on your mattress just like a regular fitted sheet. Ensure it is snug, with elastic corners tucked in.
  3. Connect the Grounding Cord: Locate the connection point on the grounding sheet. Insert the metal snap on the grounding cord into this connection point.
  4. Ground the Cord: Plug the other end into a properly grounded outlet. Verify with an outlet tester if needed.
  5. Make Skin Contact: Lie down on the grounding sheet, ensuring bare skin contact. You can place a regular sheet on top if desired.
  6. Relax and Enjoy: Enjoy the benefits of grounding as you sleep.

How do I connect the sheet to ground?

  1. Locate the Connection Point: Find the small tab/pocket on your sheet for the grounding cord.
  2. Secure the Cord: Insert the metal snap on the grounding cord into the connection point. Ensure it's secure.
  3. Plug into Outlet: Plug the other end into a properly grounded wall outlet.

Can I use a grounding sheet with other bedding?

Absolutely! You can layer it under your favorite sheets, blankets, or duvet covers.

Can I wash my grounding sheet? If so, how?

Yes, you can machine wash your ECOHEAL grounding sheet. Follow these steps:

  1. Disconnect: Unplug the grounding cord from both the sheet and the outlet.
  2. Wash: Place the sheet in your washing machine. Use cold water and a gentle cycle.
  3. Detergent: Use a mild, natural detergent free of bleach or fabric softener.
  4. Dry: Tumble dry on low heat or air dry. Avoid high heat to protect conductive fibers.

How often should I wash my grounding sheet?

Recommendation: Wash your grounding sheet every 1-2 weeks, or more often if needed, to maintain hygiene and conductivity.

Do I need to use a special outlet for my grounding sheet?
